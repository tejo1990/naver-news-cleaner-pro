# Privacy Policy for NNC - Naver News Cleaner (Pro)

**Last Updated: February 21, 2026**

NNC - Naver News Cleaner ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our Chrome extension.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
NNC is designed with a "Privacy First" philosophy.
- **No Personal Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
- **Local Processing**: All news filtering, keyword analysis, and AI analysis logic are performed locally within your browser. 
- **User Settings**: Your blocklists (sources, keywords) and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's `storage.sync` and `storage.local` APIs to provide synchronization across your signed-in devices. This data is never sent to our servers.

### 2. Permissions Justification
- **storage**: Used to save your filtering preferences and statistics locally.
- **activeTab**: Used to inject the AI analysis button and filter news content on the currently viewed Naver News page.
- **alarms**: Used to monitor the 7-day free trial period and reset daily block counts.
- **host permissions**: Necessary to identify and filter news elements on Naver domains (`*.naver.com`).

### 3. Third-Party Services
We do not share any data with third-party services. Our AI analytics (Pro feature) is powered by a local WebAssembly engine and does not require sending article content to external APIs.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

### 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **penapllee@gmail.com**
